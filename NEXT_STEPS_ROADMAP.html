Caveats

Things to know about the numbers above
  • All AUCs reported are out-of-sample, time-aware (no leakage).
  • Players without a prior-year grade are flagged No Baseline rather than imputed.
  • Forward-looking 2025 FB predictions use 2024 v7 features as proxy context until 2025 v7 features are built (priority #1 in flight).
  • 2024 PFF defense + 2025 PFF blocking were partial in earlier consolidated files. Full-season splices applied 2026-04-26.
