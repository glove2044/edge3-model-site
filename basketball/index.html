Basketball data the model sees

EDGE3 evaluates players. Players are the program’s primary asset.

Three categories of basketball data feed the EDGE3 base. Your staff adds the edge layer on top.

01 · EDGE3 base (evaluation)

What the shared model is built on.

  • HS recruiting + film · Hudl, MaxPreps, 247Sports, On3, Rivals
  • College production · Sports Reference advanced rates, KenPom, BartTorvik
  • Possession + scheme data · Synergy, NCAA box scores
  • International production · FIBA, EuroLeague, top domestic leagues
  • Transfer portal · live + historical movement
  • Performance tracking · Catapult, GPS wearables (where licensed)
  • Surveys · player, family, staff inputs
02 · Real-time indicators

What sharpens the read in-season.

  • Game-level production · rolling 7-day update
  • Minutes + role shifts · rotation moves, injury status
  • Social pattern analysis · engagement, language, activity trajectory
  • Reported buzz · coach + family conversations, visit reports

A note on social. Following kids on social media is one thing. Analyzing patterns is another. We do the second. Pattern signal, not surveillance.

03 · Your edge layer

What only your staff adds.

  • Position-coach grades and film notes
  • In-house scouting reports
  • AAU and travel evaluations
  • Recruiting visit + family relationship intel
  • Practice and weight-room evaluations
  • Any structured + unstructured data your room collects

Your data stays your data. EDGE3 never sees your edge layer across other programs. The base is ours. The edge is yours.

How we evaluate every recruit.

Three questions on every recruit. EDGE3 puts all three in one read.

01 · Your track record
Does he fit what you do?
Graded against your own past players at every HC stop. Not against the school’s pre-tenure history.
02 · Your real bar
Can he beat the top of the conference?
The bar isn’t conference average. It’s the elite programs you actually have to win against.
03 · Matchup trend
Is the trend shifting against him?
A guard who fits your past success becomes a risk if the conference is trending bigger and faster. We flag it.
