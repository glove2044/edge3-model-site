Use case · Ty Benefield

Sometimes the right call is to stay. Sometimes it’s to go. The data drives both.

Two chapters with the same athlete. The same intelligence drove opposite calls, one year apart. That’s how EDGE3 earns the family’s trust.

Chapter 1 · 2024 season at Boise State

The call: stay another year.

David Benefield (Ty’s dad) reached out after the 2024 season. Should Ty enter the portal? EDGE3 pulled the snap data on safety transfers moving from Tier 2 Power 5 programs to Tier 1 Power 4 destinations. The pattern: reduced snaps in year one, almost across the board. The contrarian recommendation: stay another year at Boise, build the tape, enter the portal from a position of strength.

What this shows: EDGE3 isn’t biased toward transferring. The data drives the call. Sometimes the smart move is to stay.

Chapter 2 · 2025 portal cycle

The call: pick the right Power 4 home.

Ty entered the portal as a top-ranked safety. Every Power 4 staff that needed a safety wanted him. EDGE3 worked alongside Ty, his agent, and his family across the named suitors: Risk/Fit Report per school. Visit question pack per school. Side-by-side scorecard. The conversation moved from “who wants me most” to “where do I fit best.”

Outcome: Ty landed at LSU. The decision was structured, the family was informed, and the school selection matched the system fit the engine surfaced.

EDGE3 didn’t replace Ty’s agent. We were the family’s intelligence layer alongside the agent. Same team. Sharper deal. Better outcome.
