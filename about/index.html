About EDGE3

Built by people who’ve been in the room.

EDGE3 was built by former pro athletes and scouts who lived inside coaching staffs and draft rooms for decades. We know how staffs evaluate. We know how families decide.

Why EDGE3 exists

Technology looking for a problem, or people who lived inside the problem?

Most AI in sports right now is technology looking for a problem. Every vendor claims AI. Most stop at dashboards. Coaches still walk into the staff meeting carrying ten reads from ten sources and synthesizing in their head.

EDGE3 was built the other way — by people who already lived inside the problem. Former pros. Scouts. People who have lived inside coaching staffs and draft rooms for decades.

Coaches know what they want. EDGE3 gets you there faster — and with more confidence.
