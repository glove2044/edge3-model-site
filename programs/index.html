How coaches actually recruit

Macro stars. Micro fit. EDGE3 sells the micro.

Most platforms sell macro evaluation. Stars. Generic projections. Big lists. Coaches recruit on micro: system fit, scheme alignment, position projection, room dynamics, institutional needs.

Macro evaluation

Stars and rankings.

What 247, On3, Rivals, and most recruiting databases sell. Public rating. National lens. Same number for every program looking.

A 5-star linebacker is a 5-star to every list. The list doesn’t tell you if he’s a 5-star for you.

Micro fit evaluation

Fit to your room.

How EDGE3 reads every athlete. Your system. Your scheme. Your current depth chart. Your developmental history. Your conference.

A 5-star at Program A can be a 2-star at Program B. EDGE3 tells you which one he is for you.
