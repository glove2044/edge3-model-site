01 📋

AI Survey Engine — Front-End Data Collection

Each school customizes their intake: position-specific questions, cultural fit criteria, behavioral assessments, academic preferences, NIL interests. The recruit (or family) fills out an interactive, branching form — think EDGE3 intake generating an immediate wallet. The survey isn't just data collection — it's the first touchpoint that makes the recruit feel valued while feeding the classification models structured inputs that traditional recruiting tools never capture.

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR — NO ONE HAS THIS
02 📡

Social Listening Agents — Continuous Public Data

Agents monitor public social profiles of every recruit in the QwikU database. Engagement trends, content sentiment, behavioral signals, brand alignment, and red flags are scored automatically. A kid's public social presence tells you things film and stats never will — maturity, brand readiness, team culture fit, risk indicators. This data feeds both the wallet and the classification models continuously.

AGENTIC — RUNS 24/7 ON AWS
03 🧠

EDGE3 Classification — Dual-Axis Scoring

Survey responses + social signals + QwikU eval data + Pro Data Stack enrichment all flow into the validated dual-axis models. RNR predicts transfer risk. RYG predicts performance outcome. The models operate across 5 positional categories — Defense, Blocking, Receiving, Rushing, Passing — trained on 26,575 classified athletes. Domain-configured AI built by practitioners who generated the data.

VALIDATED — 73.9%–91.6% ACCURACY

As new survey data, social signals, stats, or film arrives — agents update both versions automatically.
