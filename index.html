What makes EDGE3 different from a recruiting dashboard.

01 · Synthesis

Synthesis, not access.

Most platforms sell access to one or two data streams. EDGE3 fuses structured signals (rates, rankings, snaps) with unstructured context (behavior, fit, environment) into one continuous read.

The data is everywhere. The read isn’t.
02 · Models

Agentic intelligence. Past, present, and this week.

EDGE3 scouts the way coaches scout. Multi-season history. Game data refreshed weekly. Social signals from this morning. We also test the math against past seasons the model never trained on, then publish the results. Honest about what played out. Honest about what’s playing out right now.

Continuous, not retrospective. Agentic, not static.
03 · Wallets

Wallets the athletes own.

The same intelligence programs use, in the family’s hands. HS recruit, college student-athlete, transfer portal. Three wallet types, athlete-owned, portable.

No other platform puts this on the family’s side of the table.
