What makes EDGE3 different from a recruiting dashboard.

01 · Synthesis

Synthesis, not access.

Recruiting data, performance data, behavioral data. Most platforms sell access to one or two streams. EDGE3 fuses structured signals (rates, rankings, snaps) with unstructured context (behavior, fit, environment) into one continuous read.

The data is everywhere. The read isn’t.
02 · Models

Models that earn trust.

Every model is refined cycle by cycle. We publish reproducibility checks against holdout seasons we never trained on. When the cohort shifts, we recalibrate. We test the way coaches do, against the seasons that already played out.

We publish how we test. We re-tune when reality moves.
03 · Wallets

Wallets the athletes own.

The same intelligence programs use, in the family’s hands. A career digital resume, not a one-time NIL deal page. HS recruit, college student-athlete, transfer portal. Three wallet types, athlete-owned, portable.

No other platform puts this on the family’s side of the table.
